Guacamole

Guacamole is a delicious avocado based dip from Mexico. It has a bit of spice and interesting flavors and textures. I recommend serving on tacos or serving by itself to eat with tortilla chips.

  • 2 Avocados, sliced
  • 1 Roma Tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 Onion, chopped
  • 1 Clove Garlic, minced
  • 1 Serrano Pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1/4 cup Cilantro, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp Lime Juice, fresh squeezed
  • 1 Tsp Kosher Salt
  1. Put the avocados in a bowl with salt and mash with a fork.
  2. Add other items to bowl and stir to combine.
  3. Season with additional salt to taste as needed.
