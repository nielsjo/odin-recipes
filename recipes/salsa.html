Salsa Mexicana

Salsa Mexicana is a beautiful salsa the is created from ingredients that mirror the colors of the Mexican flag. It is one of the quintessential salsas and quite delightful.

  • 4 Roma Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 White Onion, chopped
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, chopped
  • 2 Jalepeno Peppers, seeded and chopped
  • 1/4 Cup Cilantro, chopped
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl, stir to combine.
  2. Add addidtional salt to taste.
