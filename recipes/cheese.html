Nacho Cheese Sauce

Nacho Cheese Sauce is incredible over chips, on tacos, or combined with other ingredients into a more complex dip.

  • 1 Tbsp Flour
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 6 Ounces Cheedar Cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 Cup Pickled Jalepeno Juice
  1. Melt butter over medium heat in saucepan.
  2. When butter begins to foam add Flour and whisk together.
  3. Cook mixture for a minute or two until it becomes a light golden color
  4. Slowly add in milk to mixture, whisking to combine.
  5. Bring milk up to a simmer and add the cheese.
  6. Once cheese is melted add the pickled jalepeno juice.
  7. Add salt to taste, serve warm.
